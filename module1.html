Module 1: Launching into Computer Science (LCS)

Launching into Computer Science module

This module is the Core Module of this course, and it will:

Investigate and explore the fundamental theories and practises of computing before progressing to trends and current developments in computer science. The module provides historical, architectural, and practical perspectives on the computer science discipline. It also allows students to engage in, experience, and imagine the discipline's current and future development. The module will teach students fundamental theoretical and practical skills and make them aware of their ethical and professional responsibilities. The module allows students to debate modern computer technology-based challenges such as global impacts, security, and threats.
