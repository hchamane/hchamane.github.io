About Hainadine

My Introduction

From Professional IT to MCs Computer Science

Hi, my name is Hainadine CHamane. I am MCs student in computer science at the University of Essex Online.

Having worked in IT for the past 23 years, I have a wealth of knowledge, various volunteer certifications, and a strong sense of teamwork. My professional background includes keeping track of system performance, troubleshooting various issues, ensuring the reliability of systems in multiple organisations, collaborating closely with coworkers, researching new technology, and successfully integrating that technology into the IT network and systems of my organisation.

Mission & Vission

My current goal is to improve my abilities so that I can meet and exceed learning objectives. Assisting in my own achievement by learning from the MCs. Computer Science course. My objective is to support the strategic plan of Essex University and develop into my personal development plan and become a qualified project manager.

Beyond computer science study, I discovered that we can develop into experts with in-depth knowledge through difficult activities, therefore I made the decision to pursue MCs using the most cutting-edge approach available: a Tough Course. In a perfect world, I'll be recognised as a top-notch IT project manager and the go-to person for advice and assistance in this area.
