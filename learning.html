Professional Skills Matrix and action plan (PDP)

Never give up. It is the world's simplest cop-out. Make a goal and stick with it until you achieve it. When you succeed in achieving it, set another one and keep going until you do. Never give up. - Bear Bryant

What do I hope to become after finishing my course?

As an IT professional, I aim to stay on top of the latest tech advancements while also monitoring the performance of IT systems. It's my responsibility to keep track of market prices for software and IT supplies, building a record for future use in my projects. My ultimate goal is to succeed as an IT Project Manager.

Goals and Outcomes

Short-term and Long-term Objectives

● Project Planning: I'm attending a two-day seminar on advanced project planning techniques in the next six months and will use these strategies in practice on my upcoming projects.

● Risk Management: I'm requesting to lead a high-risk project within the next year to learn from my team's skilled risk managers. Additionally, I plan to read two risk management books over the next six months.

● Stakeholder Management: I'm developing a plan to include stakeholders in the subsequent project and asking for their input at least twice. I'm also enrolling in a stakeholder management training program within the following year.

● Communication: I ask team members and stakeholders for input on my communication techniques every three months. Also, I'm enrolling in a public speaking course in the next six months.

● Leadership: I'm enrolling in a three-day leadership development course the following year. I'm also taking on a leadership role in a cross-functional team in the next six months.

● Budget Management: I'm requesting to manage a more complicated project budget within the next year and attend a budget management course within the next six months.

● Change Management: I'm attending a two-day change management program within the next year and offering to lead a company-wide reform initiative in the next six months.

With these objectives in mind, I'm determined to become the best IT Project Manager. I'm confident that with hard work, dedication, and a continuous desire to learn, I'll be able to achieve greatness.
