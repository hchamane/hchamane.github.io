Induction Module

This module aims to:

● Welcome and introduce students to the course.

● Getting to know the other students and learning about the University of Essex Online's virtual learning environment (VLE).

● Make students aware of materials accessible on the Study Skills Hub.

● Advise students on how to get the most out of online learning and learning communities.

● Introduce students to academic skills such as essay/report writing, research procedures, referencing, reflective learning, and critical thinking at the graduate level.

Learning Outcomes:

By the end of the Module, I was able to:

● Get to know the Virtual Learning Environment (VLE).

● Learn to use the Harvard Reference Guide.

● Have a firm grasp of course grading standards.

● I learned critical academic research rules such as wider reading, writing guides and critical analysis.

● I learned about GitHub and started researching this fantastic platform, and every day I'm learning new techniques and improving how I work with it.
